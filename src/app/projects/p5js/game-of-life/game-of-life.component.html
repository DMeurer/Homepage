Game of Life

I dont think I have to explain what this is, if you dont know it's just a Wikipedia article away. The life cells are random placed and then the simulation will be started. When I made this years ago, I didn't see a need for any interactivity or at least a restart button. So I guess you will have to reload the page to look at it again.

Please keep in mind, this is a very old project of mine. The size is not dynamic and it is not at all oriented towards mobile phones.
